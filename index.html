Firm Overview

Bay Street Capital is a vertically integrated real estate investment firm founded to acquire and execute value-add strategies on industrial properties with low building coverage and in-fill locations. The firm invests in industrial outdoor storage and industrial services facilities that are mission-critical to the logistics supply chain and construction industry.
